# Review Questions 8-10

### Question 8: Electricity vs. Electronics
* **Electricity** Flow of electrical charge through conductors.
* **Electronics** Manipulation and control of electrical current to process information or perform specific tasks using components like transistors and diodes.
* **Key Difference:** Electricity is about power and energy, whereas electronics is about control and data.

### Question 9: Electronic System Example (Smartphone)
A smartphone is a complex electronic system. Electronics contribute to its functionality through:
* **Processors (ICs):** Control all software operations.
* **Capacitors/Resistors:** Stabilize power flow to protect delicate circuits.
* **Sensors:** Convert physical touch or light into electrical signals for the device to process.

### Question 10: Scope and Applications
* **Electricity Scope:** Large-scale power generation and distribution. 
    * *Examples:* Electric motors, home wiring.
* **Electronics Scope:** Small-scale signal processing and automated control.
    * *Examples:* Smart phones, Computers.
